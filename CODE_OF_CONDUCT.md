# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want XiChuan Blog to be a practical and respectful project for people building their own publishing systems. Be direct, constructive, and considerate when discussing bugs, ideas, and code.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus criticism on technical facts and project outcomes.
- Respect different experience levels and deployment constraints.
- Keep discussions relevant to the issue or pull request.
- Report security-sensitive concerns privately according to [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or sustained personal attacks.
- Publishing another person’s private information.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussions.
- Sharing secrets or exploit details publicly when private reporting is appropriate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
